DINING

Breakfasts

Start your day at Paradise with a refreshing breakfast featuring a colorful selection of fresh fruits, warm artisan breads, and a variety of healthy meals. From energizing smoothies to wholesome grain bowls, every bite is crafted to nourish your body and brighten your morning.

Breakfast 7:00 am-11:00 am

Lunch

Savor a delicious midday escape with our lunch offerings at Paradise. Choose from a vibrant à la carte menu or indulge in our generous lunch buffet, featuring a variety of fresh salads, international dishes, grilled favorites, and decadent desserts — all prepared with the finest ingredients to satisfy every craving.

Lunch 12:00 pm-3:00 pm

Tea Time

Relax and indulge during tea time at Paradise, where a delightful high tea buffet awaits. Enjoy a selection of delicate pastries, freshly baked cakes, and classic treats, perfectly paired with a fine assortment of teas for a sweet and elegant afternoon escape.

Tea Time 4:00 pm-6:00 pm

Dinner

End your day at Paradise with a delightful dinner buffet featuring a variety of healthy meals, fresh salads, grilled specialties, and flavorful international dishes. Enjoy a relaxed, elegant atmosphere where every bite is crafted to nourish and satisfy.

Dinner 7:00 pm-11:00 pm
